About Me


I am currently a senior research engineer in the Learning Robot Department of Robot Laboratory at FANUC Corporation. I was a Ph.D student and then a postdoctoral scholar in the Mechanical Systems Control Lab in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley. I worked with Professor Masayoshi Tomizuka on the several research projects including "A Hybrid Control Systems Approach to Brain-Machine Interfaces for Exoskeleton Control" and "Intelligent Control of Robot Manipulators for Performance Enhancement".

I obtained my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees both in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley in May 2009 and August 2012, respectively. Prior to coming to UC Berkeley, I was an undergraduate student in Zhejiang University majored in Mechatronic Engineering and also hornored in Advanced Class of Engineering Education (ACEE), where I got my Bachelor degree in June 2007.
