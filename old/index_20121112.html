About Me


I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow in the Mechanical Systems Control Lab in the Dept. of Mechanical Engineering at UC. Berkeley. I am working with Professor Masayoshi Tomizuka on the several research projects including "Exoskeleton design and control for brain-machine interfaces" and "Intelligent control of robot manipulators for performance enhancement".

I obtained my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees both in Mechanical Engineering from UC. Berkeley in May 2009 and August 2012, respectively. Prior to coming to UC. Berkeley, I was an undergraduate student in Zhejiang University majored in Mechatronic Engineering and also hornored in Advanced Class of Engineering Education (ACEE), where I got my Bachelor degree in June 2007.
